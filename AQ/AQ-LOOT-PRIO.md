# AQ LOOT PRIO

## T2,5 PRIO:

### HEAD:
#### Emperor Vek'lor (Paladin, Hunter, Rogue, Druid)
    Rogue > others
#### Emperor Vek'nilash (Warrior, Warlock, Mage, Priest)
    Warrior (tank) > Warlock  > Mage (without Mish'undare) > Mage (with Mish'undare) > Warrior (dps) > Priest

### SHOULDER & BOOTS:
#### Viscidus & Princess Huhuran:
##### (Warrior, Hunter, Rogue, Priest): 
    Warrior (tank + dps) shoulders > Rogue shoulders > Rogue boots (without Shadow Flame) > Rogue boots (with Shadow Flame) > Warrior boots > others
##### (Paladin, Mage, Warlock, Druid): 
    Warlock shoulders > ?

### LEGS:
#### Ouro:
##### (Warrior, Rogue, Priest, Mage): 
    Warrior (tank) > Warrior (dps) > Rogue > Mage (AQ20 legs better) > Priest
##### (Paladin, Hunter, Warlock, Druid): 
    MS/OS? Warlock (AQ20 legs better). Decent Feral Legs.

### CHEST:
#### C'Thun:
##### (Warrior, Paladin, Hunter, Rogue)
    Warrior (tank) > Rogue > Warrior (dps) (Breast of Annihilation better) > others
##### (Priest, Mage, Warlock, Druid)
    Warlock/Mage > Shadow Priest > others

#### Bad upgrades for following classes:
    Paladin (only for ret)
    Priest (only for shadow priest)
    Hunter

---

### EYE OF C'THUN:
    Warrior (tank) > Casters > Healers > Hunters > others

### IMPERIAL QIRAJI REGALIA:
    Caster / Healer that needs it > Warrior (tank) > others

### IMPERIAL QIRAJI ARMAMENTS:
    Rogue (dagger) / Warrior (dps non-human) > others
